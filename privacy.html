Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 2, 2026
Last Updated: February 2, 2026

IMPORTANT NOTICE REGARDING CHILDREN

Nighty is designed for family use under adult supervision. We do NOT record children's voices. Only adults (parents/guardians) provide voice samples for the app's voice cloning feature.

Age Requirements

  • Account Creation: Must be 18 years or older
  • Child Usage: Children may use the app only under direct parental/guardian supervision
  • Voice Recording: Only adults (18+) may record voice samples

1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

1.1 Personal Information

  • Account Information: Email address, name, billing information (processed by Apple)
  • Voice Data: Adult voice recordings for voice cloning (with explicit consent)
  • Usage Data: App interactions, feature usage, crash logs
  • Device Information: Device model, iOS version, app version, device identifiers

1.2 Generated Content

  • Stories: AI-generated bedtime stories based on user inputs
  • Images: AI-generated illustrations for stories
  • Audio: Text-to-speech audio using cloned voices

1.3 What We DO NOT Collect

  • Children's Voices: We never record or store children's voice data
  • Location Data: We do not collect precise location information
  • Contacts: We do not access your device contacts
  • Photos/Camera: Except for profile pictures (optional, with permission)

2. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

2.1 Primary Uses

  • Voice Cloning: Process adult voice samples to create personalized text-to-speech
  • Story Generation: Create custom bedtime stories using AI technology
  • Service Delivery: Provide app functionality and features
  • Account Management: Maintain your subscription and preferences

2.2 Secondary Uses

  • Improvement: Analyze usage patterns to improve app features
  • Support: Provide customer service and technical support
  • Legal Compliance: Meet legal obligations and enforce our terms

3. DATA PROCESSING AND STORAGE LOCATIONS

3.1 Data Storage (United States)

  • Primary Database: Supabase (US-hosted servers)
  • Media Storage: Cloudflare R2 (US-hosted)
  • Backup Systems: US-based cloud infrastructure

3.2 AI Processing (Singapore)

  • Voice Cloning: Alibaba DashScope servers in Singapore
  • Text-to-Speech: DashScope TTS processing in Singapore
  • Data Transfer: Voice samples transmitted securely for AI processing

3.3 Cross-Border Data Transfers

We implement appropriate safeguards for international data transfers, including:

  • Encryption in transit and at rest
  • Standard contractual clauses with third-party processors
  • Regular security audits and compliance monitoring

4. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

4.1 AI and Processing Services

  • Alibaba DashScope (Singapore): Voice cloning and text-to-speech
  • Alibaba DashScope (Qwen): Story generation
  • fal.ai: Story illustrations

4.2 Infrastructure and Analytics

  • Supabase: Database and authentication services
  • Cloudflare R2: File storage and content delivery
  • Apple App Store: Payment processing and app distribution
  • RevenueCat: Subscription management and analytics

4.3 Third-Party Privacy

Each third-party service operates under their own privacy policies. We require all processors to maintain appropriate data protection standards.

5. DATA RETENTION AND DELETION

5.1 Voice Data Retention

  • Voice Samples: Retained as long as your account is active
  • Cloned Voice Models: Deleted within 30 days of account deletion
  • Generated Audio: Cached for performance, deleted after 90 days

5.2 Account Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: Data retained while subscription is active
  • Deleted Accounts: Most data deleted within 30 days
  • Legal Requirements: Some data may be retained longer for legal compliance

5.3 Automatic Deletion

  • Inactive Accounts: Data deleted after 2 years of inactivity
  • Failed Payments: Account data retained for 90 days after payment failure

  • Access: Request copies of your personal data

6.2 California Residents (CCPA/CPRA)

  • Right to Know: Categories and specific pieces of data collected
  • Right to Delete: Deletion of personal information
  • Right to Opt-Out: Opt out of data sales (we do not sell data)
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: Equal service regardless of privacy choices

6.3 European Residents (GDPR)

  • Withdrawal of Consent: Withdraw consent for voice processing
  • Data Protection Officer: Contact details below
  • Supervisory Authority: Right to lodge complaints

7. DATA SECURITY

7.1 Technical Safeguards

  • Encryption: AES-256 encryption for data at rest
  • Transmission: TLS 1.3 for data in transit
  • Access Controls: Role-based access with multi-factor authentication
  • Regular Audits: Security assessments and penetration testing

7.2 Organizational Measures

  • Employee Training: Privacy and security awareness programs
  • Incident Response: Breach detection and response procedures
  • Data Minimization: Collect only necessary data
  • Purpose Limitation: Use data only for stated purposes

8. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY (COPPA COMPLIANCE)

8.1 No Collection from Children

  • We do NOT knowingly collect personal information from children under 13
  • Voice recording feature is restricted to adults (18+) only
  • Children may use the app only under parental supervision

8.2 Parental Controls

8.3 If We Learn of Child Data Collection
